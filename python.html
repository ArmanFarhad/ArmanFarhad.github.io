Python: Become Human

Become Human is a emotional story about a man named Hank that is a detective for the Chicago Police Department.
All was good until his wife went to get groceries and she was gunned down. Hank attempts to find the killer and
bring them to justice... or does he? Some programming concepts I learned and applied to this story were the if
statements. The if statements played an important role because it managed which scene would be showing next, depending on
the choice that was selected. Another concept I applied to this story was functions.
This allowed me to code in a much cleaner manor and was able to identify each scene.
Thinking back to the stories that I read, the one that peaked my interest is Jeremy's. It had a great story
with many choices that the user could branch out to.
